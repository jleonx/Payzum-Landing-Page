Why Use Payzum Payment?

2Factor Authentication

Security will always be a top priority in every decision we make, We provide access to a second factor authentication to enable you fully secure your accounts. We also provide other security features that make our service highly secure

Crypto Settlements

We provide Crypto settlements at the end of every business day if the merchant wishes so

Hedging Services

A merchant has the option to request for hedging services whereby fiat settlements are made at the exact values paid to the merchant. This is so you do not have to worry about the volatility of the bitcoin market

Efficient support

We are always willing to to answer your questions and guide you through the use of our services. We are flexible and can tweak our services to suit your business needs

How to get Payzum Payment ?

Free Register

Creating your account is simple, once you click on the "register" button at the top right corner of the page, you would see a simple registration form, once that is completed submit id for verification and enable 2FA to use all our features effectively.

KYC Verify

KYC check is the mandatory process of identifying and verifying the identity of the client when opening an account and periodically over time.

Wallet & Withdrawals

The wallets are automatically created and can be used to store cryptocurrency and withdraw at will. For those using our hedging service, we would always pay fiat based on current values at payment time.

Merchant Use

Click on the ''merchant tools'' to use features such as our simple buttons to place on your site for payment and our API and IPN features. More details are given in that section
